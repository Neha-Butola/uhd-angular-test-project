About University of Houston–Downtown

UHD is a dynamic university offering bachelor’s and master’s degrees at multiple locations across Houston and online. You can design your future from an academic program that sparks your passion and connects you with fellow students, businesses and the community.

When you step onto our campus it’s like walking into the heart of downtown Houston. You’re just steps away from Fortune 500 companies, world-renowned arts organizations, award-winning restaurants, exciting entertainment and professional sports. UHD is woven into the vibrant streets of the fourth largest city in the United States and has urban appeal that celebrates the multicultural vibe of Houston.
